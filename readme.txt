DOOM TOURNAMENT

What began as a test for importing UT vertex meshes is turning into an actual
full UT weapon and item mod.

Currently implemented:

 - Flak Cannon (slot 8)
 - ASMD Shock Rifle (slot 4)
 - Redeemer (slot 0)
 - GES Biorifle (slot 3)

In progress:

 - Pulsegun (slot 5) (with beta animations)
